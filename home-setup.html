Home Setup Guide

This guide will walk you through the steps needed to set up your Parking Management System at home. Here, you'll learn about the necessary equipment, software installation, and initial configuration settings.

Equipment Needed

  • High-resolution cameras
  • PC with adequate specifications
  • Reliable internet connection

Installation Steps

  1. Mount the cameras at strategic locations.
  2. Install the necessary software on your PC.
  3. Connect the cameras to your PC via your network.
  4. Configure the software to recognize the cameras.
  5. Test the system to ensure it works properly.

Configuration Tips

Make sure to adjust the camera angles to cover the maximum area of your parking space. Adjust settings for optimal video quality depending on your hardware and environmental conditions.
